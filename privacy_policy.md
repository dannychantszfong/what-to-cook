# Privacy Policy for What to Cook

**Last updated: 2025/6/27**

## Information We Collect
- **Ingredients and recipes**: We temporarily send your ingredient lists to AI services to generate recipes
- **App preferences**: We store your AI model preferences and API keys locally on your device

## How We Use Information
- **Recipe Generation**: Your ingredients are sent to third-party AI services (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.) to generate personalized recipes
- **App Functionality**: Your preferences are stored locally to improve your experience

## Data Storage
- **Local Storage**: API keys and preferences are stored locally on your device
- **No Cloud Storage**: We do not store your personal data on our servers
- **Third-party Services**: AI providers may temporarily process your ingredient lists according to their privacy policies

## Data Sharing
- We only share ingredient information with AI services you choose to use
- We do not sell or share your personal information with other parties

## Your Rights
- You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app
- You control which AI service to use
- You can modify or delete your stored preferences at any time
